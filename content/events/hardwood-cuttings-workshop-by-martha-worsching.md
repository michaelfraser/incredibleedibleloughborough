# Hardwood Cuttings Workshop by Martha Worsching

10/01/2026 at Fearon Hall

Hardwood cuttings are best taken at the time when plants are dormant, i.e. between November and February/March. The following plants are ideal for propagating at this time of year: bay, currant black/red/white, fig, forsythia, gooseberry, grape vine, roses, hawthorn, hazel.

This is a hands-on workshop in which Martha Worsching demonstrates how to propagate plants from cuttings.
