# Where & When We Garden

Come and garden with us. Join us at our different [gardens ](/our-gardens/)throughout the week. Everyone welcome — whether you stay 20 minutes or more, want to just chat, maybe see what we're doing or have any questions. **[See here where we are.](/our-gardens/)**

## From March 2026

### Mondays 6pm - 8pm

* Thorpe Acre Road 
* Warwick Way Flats

### Wednesdays 2pm - 4pm

* Knightthorpe Methodist Church

### Wednesdays 5pm - 7pm 

* King George Road Allotment

### Saturdays 10am - 12am

* King George Road Allotment or gardens around Fearon Hall

#### Do I need experience?

Nope! All skill levels are welcome.

#### Do I need to sign up?

No sign-up needed — these are drop-in sessions. We'd love to meet you.

#### What should I bring?

Wear clothes you don’t mind getting dirty. Tools and guidance are provided.
